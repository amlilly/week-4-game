Crystals Collector

Instructions

A random target score number is provided at the start of the game and each successive round.

Four magical crystals are shown below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The value of each crystal, however, is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target score number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the target score number.
